The Equation

\[\text{outcome}_{ij} = \beta_0 + \beta_1 \text{parental warmth} + \beta_2 \text{physical punishment} + \beta_3 \text{time} + \]

\[\beta_4 \text{group}_2 + \beta_5 HDI +\]

\[u_{0j} + u_{1j} \times \text{parental warmth} + e_{ij} \]
